    • Unlock Pi Wallet

    Many people have generated their pi wallet through Pi browser and didn’t save their Passphrase and lost it. Now they can't open their Pi wallet because they have forgotten their Passphrase and the Pi wallet has been locked now. In this article I will tell how can you recover and reset your passphrase and then you can get and save your passphrase.

    1. Step 1: We will open the Pi Network application.

      Step 2: Click on the 3 lines on top left side of the application and click on the Pi browser.

      Step 3: When we click on the Pi browser, we will see the sign in interface to the browser. Click on the sign in to the browser and direct access to the Pi browser. If you don’t have the pi browser then download from the play store. After that, the browser will open with different options such as Chat.Pi, Wallet.Pi, Brainstorm.pi, mine.Pi, blockchain.pi, develop.pi and kyc.pi

      Step 4: Click on ‘Wallet.Pi’ and you can see two options on the screen, ‘Unlock with Passphrase’ and ‘Unlock with Fingerprint’. The issue will rise here for those who haven’t saved or deleted their passphrase, when you press on ‘Unlock with passphrase’ then error comes because we don’t have the passphrase.
